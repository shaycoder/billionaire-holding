Tremendous Growth of Wellness Industry

In 2022, the global wellness market hit $5.59 trillion. It is expected to reach nearly $10 trillion in next five years. Wellness Industry is the fastest growing industry with a growth rate of 12% to 20 % annually.

Wellness is beyond physical health, the concept of wellness now firmly includes mental health, physical fitness, nutrition, healthy eating, organic food, sleep and mindfulness - essentially a more wholesome approach to healthy living.

The wellness industry can play a vital role in helping to facilitate preventative lifestyle and behavior practices that address these challenges and create positive social outcomes.

Wellness is commonly viewed as having seven dimensions: mental, physical, social, financial, spiritual, environmental and vocational. These dimensions are interdependent and influence each other.
